Exam Seat Arrangement System

The Exam Seating Arrangement System is a web application that automatically arranges students for exams in a fair and equitable manner, while also preventing malpractice. The system uses a algorithm and heuristics to arrange students, ensuring that they are evenly distributed across the exam rooms and that they are not able to cheat. The system is designed to be used by all departments and years at the university, and it is expected to save time and resources for staff.
